# Creator Starter

<!-- Use during onboarding Phase 2 when the founder runs a content business: newsletter, YouTube, courses, community. -->

## venture.md skeleton

- **Product:** <the content + the thing that makes money: sponsorships, courses, memberships, digital products>
- **Customers / target users:** <the audience: who they are, what transformation they want>
- **Revenue model:** <sponsorship / course sales / memberships / affiliate, with current split>
- **Current numbers:** subscribers/followers per channel, open or view rates, revenue per stream, with as-of dates
- **Top 3 priorities (as of YYYY-MM-DD):**
  1.
  2.
  3.
- **Biggest risk right now:** <usually: platform dependency or the founder being the bottleneck for all production>

## Vault taxonomy that fits creators

- `vault/content/`: published pieces (canonical), one subfolder per channel
- `vault/pipeline/`: drafts, idea backlog, content calendar
- `vault/audience/`: subscriber exports, survey results, best-performing topics analysis
- `vault/sponsors/`: rate card, sponsor contacts, past deals
- `vault/products/`: course materials, digital product files

## Numbers that matter

Subscriber delta per week, one engagement rate per channel, revenue per stream, and email list growth (the only audience you own). Follower totals are vanity; deltas are signal.

## Typical first open loops

- Idea backlog lives in 4 places, consolidate into pipeline
- No rate card exists, sponsors get quoted ad-hoc
- Email list export is 3 months stale, refresh and dedupe
