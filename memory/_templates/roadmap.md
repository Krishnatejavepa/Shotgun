## Stage: Idea
- [ ] Sharpen the idea into one sentence (who, pain, product) | owner: IDEA | evidence: memory/venture.md filled
- [ ] Talk to 5 potential customers about the pain | owner: RESEARCH | evidence: notes in vault/research/
- [ ] Check what already exists (competitors, pricing) | owner: RESEARCH | evidence: dated competitor note in vault/research/

## Stage: Setup
- [ ] Pick the company/product name | owner: DECIDE | evidence: memory/decisions.md entry
- [ ] Jurisdiction + legal shape decided (incorporate or wait, visa constraints checked) | owner: LEGAL | evidence: memory/legal-compliance.md
- [ ] Repo + hosting rails up (staging → prod) | owner: STACK | evidence: STACK STATUS hosting: ready

## Stage: Identity
- [ ] Brand kit locked (vibe, palette, type) | owner: DESIGN | evidence: vault/brand/DESIGN.md
- [ ] Domain bought and pointed | owner: STACK | evidence: STACK STATUS domain: ready
- [ ] Landing page live saying what it is and for whom | owner: BUILD | evidence: URL in memory/venture.md

## Stage: Build
- [ ] Define the smallest version that proves the value | owner: DECIDE | evidence: memory/decisions.md entry
- [ ] Build it | owner: BUILD | evidence: workspace project, changelog line
- [ ] First metric wired (one number, tracked weekly) | owner: STACK | evidence: memory/metrics.md rows exist

## Stage: Go-to-market
- [ ] ICP defined (who buys, why now, disqualifiers) | owner: SELL | evidence: decision entry + venture.md
- [ ] One distribution channel chosen and started | owner: GROW | evidence: decision entry + growth-log
- [ ] First 10 real conversations with the target customer | owner: SELL | evidence: vault/crm/accounts.md

## Stage: Launch
- [ ] Payments live (someone CAN pay) | owner: STACK | evidence: STACK STATUS payments: ready
- [ ] Launch assets ready (post, page, email) in the founder's voice | owner: WRITE | evidence: files in vault/marketing/
- [ ] Launch executed, Panel-reviewed first | owner: GROW | evidence: changelog [external] line
- [ ] Legal basics live (privacy policy, terms) | owner: LEGAL | evidence: files in vault/legal/

## Stage: Scale
- [ ] First paying customer | owner: SELL | evidence: memory/metrics.md revenue row
- [ ] Support loop running (feedback triaged weekly) | owner: SUPPORT | evidence: routine in memory/routines.md
- [ ] Growth experiments running on a rhythm | owner: GROW | evidence: growth-log entries with verdicts
- [ ] Unit economics understood (what a customer costs vs brings) | owner: FINANCE | evidence: vault/finance/ doc

## Stage: Mature
- [ ] Pricing revisited against real data | owner: DECIDE | evidence: decision entry
- [ ] The ONE metric grows month over month | owner: DAILY | evidence: memory/metrics.md trend
- [ ] Founder time goes to the top 3 priorities, the rest is delegated or dropped | owner: DAILY | evidence: weekly reviews say so
