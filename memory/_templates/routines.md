## Routine: Morning standup

## Routine: Weekly review
- Cadence: weekly (pick a day)
- Criteria: none
- What: weekly review, or CEO Weekly for the full ritual (.shotgun/skills/daily/SKILL.md)
- Last run: never
- State: active

## Routine: System checkup
- Cadence: monthly (first weekly review of the month)
- Criteria: none
- What: full doctor checkup with Health Score (.shotgun/skills/doctor/SKILL.md)
- Last run: never
- State: active
